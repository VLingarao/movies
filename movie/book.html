JUNGLE BOOK Movie

Each story is followed by a poem that serves as an epigram. A boy is raised by wolves in the Indian jungle with the help of Baloo
the bear and Bagheera the black panther, who teach him the "Law of the Jungle". Some years later, the wolfpack and Mowgli
are threatened by the tiger Shere Khan.
