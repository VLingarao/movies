JAILER Movie

Jailer (2023 Malayalam film) is about a prison officer who lives with five prisoners in a bungalow in the 1950s. The film stars
Dhyan Sreenivasan as the prison officer, and also features Manoj K. Jayan, Divya Pillai, and Sreejith Ravi.His son, ACP
Arjun (Vasanth Ravi), is an Assistant Commissioner of Police who investigates a gangster who smuggles idols.
