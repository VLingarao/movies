LEO movie

Leo is a 2023 Indian film and the third installment in the Lokesh Cinematic Universe. The film is inspired by A History of Violence.
It stars Thalapathy Vijay as Parthi, a cafe owner and animal rescuer in Theog, Himachal Pradesh. Parthi's life is disturbed when gangsters
Antony and Harold Das suspect him of being Antony's estranged son, Leo.

The cast of the 2023 movie LEO includes:
