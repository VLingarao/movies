PUSHPA Movie

The story of Pushpa: The Rise is about a laborer named Pushpa Raj who rises through the ranks of a red sandalwood smuggling
syndicate in the late 1990s. Pushpa is denied a surname because of his bastard status and is constantly disrespected by his "brothers"
and the society around him in Chittoor District.

The cast of the 2021 movie PUSHPA includes:

  1. Allu Arjun.
  2. Rashmika Mandanna.
  3. Jagadeesh Bandari.
  4. Sunil Varma.
  5. Dhananjay.
  6. Ajay Ghosh.
  7. Anasuya Bharadwaj.
  8. Bhanwar Singh Shekhawat.
