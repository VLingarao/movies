RRR Movie

RRR is a 2022 movie about two revolutionaries and a British officer who team up to fight against the British in the 1920s.
The movie is set in pre-independence Delhi and loosely based on the lives of two real-life Indian revolutionaries,
Komaram Bheem and Alluri Sitarama Raju. Ram Charan plays Rama Raju and Jr NTR (N. T. Rama Rao, Jr.) plays Komaram Bheem.
