Vikram Movie

Vikram, a former commander of the black-ops squad's pilot batch, who sets to take down Sandhanam, the runner of a drug
syndicate named Vetti Vagaiyara. A special agent investigates a murder committed by a masked group of serial killers. However,
a tangled maze of clues soon leads him to the drug kingpin of Chennai.
